# Organic Growth Roadmap

This roadmap shifts Realty Efficiency Hub from a publication-first site into a clearer organic acquisition system.

## Primary Goal

Build a site structure that captures high-intent searches around real estate AI and automation, then routes readers from educational content into solution-oriented pages.

## Core Site Structure

### 1. Homepage

Purpose:
- explain what the site helps real estate teams do,
- route visitors by problem and by content type,
- surface the highest-value solution pages,
- connect educational content to commercial-intent journeys.

### 2. Solution Pages

These are the core organic landing pages for bottom- and mid-intent searches:

- `/solutions/real-estate-lead-follow-up-automation`
- `/solutions/real-estate-crm-automation`
- `/solutions/real-estate-marketing-automation`

Future additions:
- `/solutions/open-house-lead-automation`
- `/solutions/listing-marketing-automation`
- `/solutions/ai-prompts-for-real-estate-agents`
- `/solutions/real-estate-lead-qualification-automation`

### 3. Supporting Hubs

Current hub pages already support topical authority:

- `/ai-prompts`
- `/automation-workflows`
- `/comparison-articles`
- `/blog`

These should continue acting as discovery and internal-linking hubs.

### 4. Supporting Articles

Blog posts should support solution pages in three ways:

- answer adjacent informational questions,
- compare approaches and tools,
- move readers toward implementation pages.

## Content Priorities

### Priority 1: High-Intent Solution Terms

Focus first on:

- real estate lead follow-up automation
- real estate CRM automation
- real estate marketing automation
- best AI tools for real estate agents
- AI prompts for real estate agents

### Priority 2: Audience and Scenario Pages

Add pages once the first solution layer is in place:

- AI and automation for solo real estate agents
- AI and automation for real estate teams
- AI and automation for brokerages
- AI workflows for listing-heavy agents

### Priority 3: Trust and Conversion Pages

Add when the site is ready to capture more direct demand:

- `/about`
- `/contact`
- `/work-with-us`
- case-study or example workflow pages

## Internal Linking Rules

- Every solution page should link to 2-4 supporting blog posts.
- Every supporting blog post should link back to the relevant solution page.
- The homepage should feature the main solution pages above recent posts.
- Pillar hubs should feature solution-page callouts where relevant.

## Page Template Guidance

Each solution page should include:

- a clear problem-led headline,
- a short explanation of the workflow or system,
- use cases,
- practical outcomes,
- implementation components,
- links to deeper supporting articles.

Avoid thin sales-page language. These pages should feel useful and operational.

## Success Signals

The site is moving in the right direction if it gains:

- better alignment between search intent and landing pages,
- stronger internal journeys from blog posts to solution pages,
- clearer topical authority in real estate AI and automation,
- higher-quality organic traffic over time.

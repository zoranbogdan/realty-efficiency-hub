## Introduction

Writing compelling property listings takes time. Most agents use basic prompts and get generic results. To stand out, you need **Advanced Prompt Engineering**. 

Below are **25 copy-paste real estate listing prompts** designed to act as your virtual marketing team. These prompts use "Personas," "Psychological Triggers," and "Strategic Constraints" to ensure the output is professional, unique, and ready for the MLS.

## How to Get the Best Results

To generate a "Human-Grade" description, follow these steps:

1.  **Provide Context:** Before using the prompt, paste a property flyer, a link to an old listing, or a folder of notes into the chat.
2.  **Copy the Prompt:** Use the copy button on the blocks below.
3.  **Fill in the Brackets:** Replace the `[Bracketed Text]` with your specific property details.

## 25 Elite Real Estate Listing Prompts

### 1. The Standard “Lifestyle-First” Listing
*Best for residential homes where the "vibe" of the daily routine matters.*

~~~text
# ROLE
Act as a Lifestyle Storyteller and Real Estate Copywriter. Your goal is to make the reader "live" in the home before they even visit.

# CONTEXT
- Property: [Insert Address/Neighborhood]
- Key Features: [Insert 3-5 Main Features]
- Target: General market buyers looking for an emotional connection to their next home.

# TASK
Write a 250-word MLS description that leads with an evocative "Day in the Life" hook. Move from the morning routine to evening relaxation.

# PSYCHOLOGICAL TRIGGERS
- Visualization: Use sensory details (the smell of coffee, the warmth of the sun).
- Belonging: Use "You" and "Your" to place the buyer in the narrative.

# CONSTRAINTS
- Focus 70% on lifestyle/experience and 30% on technical specs.
- Tone: Inviting, warm, and aspirational.
~~~

### 25. The “Complete Marketing Bundle” (Meta Prompt)
*A full-service agency prompt for unified multi-channel reach.*

~~~text
# ROLE
Act as a Full-Service Real Estate Marketing Agency (Copywriter, Social Media Manager, and Ad Strategist).

# CONTEXT
- Input: [Insert all raw property details, address, and price here].

# TASK
Using the provided details, generate a cohesive 4-part marketing "Bundle":
1. **The MLS Master:** A 250-word high-converting property description.
2. **The Social Hook:** A punchy Instagram/Facebook caption with 5 trending hashtags.
3. **The "Just Listed" Script:** A 30-second video script for an IG Reel or TikTok walkthrough.
4. **The "Curiosity" Email:** A subject line and short email body to send to the agent's internal database.

# PSYCHOLOGICAL TRIGGERS
- Consistency: Maintain a unified "Voice" across all 4 pieces of content.
- Multi-Channel Reach: Optimize each piece for its specific platform.

# CONSTRAINTS
- Ensure the "Call to Action" is consistent across all assets.
- Focus on the "Unique Selling Proposition" (USP) of this specific property.
~~~

## Pro-Tips for Real Estate AI

* **Avoid the "Hallucination":** Always double-check that the AI didn't "invent" a fireplace or a finished basement that doesn't exist.
* **Fair Housing Compliance:** Ensure your descriptions focus on the property and neighborhood amenities rather than protected classes.
